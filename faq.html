FAQs

Helpful answers before you call

A few common questions about True Care Home Support and the services offered.

Do you provide personal care?

At present, True Care provides non-medical home support only. We do not provide regulated personal care or nursing care unless properly registered to do so.

What areas do you cover?

Swansea, Port Talbot, Neath and surrounding areas.

Can I arrange a free consultation?

Yes. You can call or message to arrange a friendly, no-obligation conversation.

Are you DBS checked and insured?

Yes. True Care is Enhanced DBS checked and fully insured.
